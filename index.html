Loc Hoang

About Me

I graduated with my Ph.D. under the supervision of Dr. Keshav Pingali in August 2021. During my Ph.D. years, I mainly worked in distributed graph analytics and associated algorithms; near the end of my Ph.D. study I focused mainly on graph neural networks.

I received both my Bachelor's of Science and my Master's of Science in Computer Science in Spring 2017 at The University of Texas at Austin under the Integrated BS/MS program in the Department of Computer Science.
